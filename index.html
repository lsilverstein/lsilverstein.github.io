My path to data science

I have many degrees. Probably too many. I started with a bachelor of fine arts in the film program at Ryerson University. After graduating I spent a few years shooting event videos while filming creative projects on the side. In 2013, on a bit of a whim, I ended up doing an MBA at Ryerson University.

After completing the MBA, I was about the enroll in a masters of economics at Ryerson, when I landed a web development job at a small start up. After working a few years in the industry, I decided to go back to school for an undergraduate computer science degree from, where else, Ryerson.

While enrolled in my second undergrad, I started meeting with professors to figure out what direction I should take after graduating. Dr. Sadeghian was kind enough to offer me a spot in his machine learning lab, and I've been working with PhD student Alex Dela Cruz on medical related machine learning projects this past year.
